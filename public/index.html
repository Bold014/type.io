Words march toward your base from the right. Type them to destroy them before they arrive. Survive as many waves as you can. Every 5 waves, a boss appears.

ENEMY TYPES

New enemy types appear as you progress through waves.

BASIC wave 1+

Standard enemy. One word, normal speed.

TANK wave 6+

Slow but uses long, hard words.

SPRINTER wave 6+

Moves at double speed. Short, easy words.

SCRAMBLED wave 11+

Letters are shuffled. Type what you see.

CHAOS wave 16+

RaNdOm capitalization. Match it exactly.

SPLITTER wave 16+

Splits into 2 smaller enemies when killed.

BOSS every 5

Appears every 5 waves. A full sentence you must type out.

UPGRADES

SLOW FIELD

All enemies move 15% slower. Stackable.

EXTRA LIFE

+1 base life. Stackable.

CHAIN LIGHTNING

Kills deal 1 damage to the nearest nearby enemy.

SHIELD

Blocks the next enemy that reaches your base. Stackable.

COMBO MASTER

+10 bonus score per consecutive kill. Stackable.

WORD SHORTEN

15% chance enemies spawn with shorter words. Stackable.
